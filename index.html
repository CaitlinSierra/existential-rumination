MORTAL COGNIZANCE

by. Caitlin Fettig

An experiential framework derived from the realization that each and every individual remains at the disposition of a greater entity to which they must acknowledge- rather than at the disposition of their bio-psycho-social environment.

When fully understood, the individual begins to realize that, due to the fall of man they have received a state of mortality. This state induces feelings of destitution, which many psychoanalytic methods fail to resolve.
